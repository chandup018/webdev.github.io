SIR M.Visvesvaraya Institute of Technology

Sir M. Visvesvaraya Institute of Technology (Sir MVIT) is a well-regarded engineering and management college in Bengaluru, Karnataka, established in 1986 by the Sri Krishnadevaraya Educational Trust, offering various UG/PG courses (B.E., M.Tech, MBA, MCA) affiliated with VTU, known for its focus on technical skills, industry exposure, research, and NAAC "A" grade accreditation.
